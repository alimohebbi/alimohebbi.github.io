Who Am I?

Hi I'm Ali Mohebbi I am a Ph.D. student in Informatics at Università della Svizzera italiana (Lugano, Switzerland) under the supervision of prof. Mauro Pezzè. My projects focus on the automatic generation of GUI test case for Interactive Application such as Android apps. In my research the goal is to generate test case that are semantically meaningful by leveraging information available in the GUI.
